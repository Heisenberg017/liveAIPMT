About Us

domain E, founded in 2006, was started for Class XII Board Exam Preparation, catering to the students preparing for Competitive Examinations at various institutes of Kota. Since ten years of its inception, domain E has successfully registered more than 17500 students for Class XII Board Exam Preparation alone. These include primarily offine (Kota centre) and online students (Live Coaching via internet). Today domain E is arguably the leader in coaching for Board Exams.
